Happy Teacher's Day

It is with eagerness that we celebrate this special day, Teacher's Day,
A day on which our teachers, will be given an appreciation note, to show our gratitude.
To other staff members of this establishment that may not have the title of "teacher", we still appreciate your work,
(Thanks for the person who fixed our door, sorry for this inconvenience, and sorry for our class being so unclean, I don't know why exactly, but we will see how the class is kept for the next days, hopefully cleaner)
A day on which we thank you for all you do to guide us on a path of uncertainty.
