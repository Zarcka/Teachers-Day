Happy Teacher's Day

I recognize today that Teachers are undoubtedly the backbone of the society.
They play a big role in building the character of students and teach them how to harness life to the fullest.

Thank you teacher, for being selfless, always devoted and hardworking to shape the society and I realise how noble you are in inspiring others.

Dear Teacher, without you, we would have been lost.
Because of your proper vision, knowledge and experience, intertwined with your willingness to be present by us at all times, you have given us all the reasons to dream big.

You may not have a big bank balance Sir/Madam, but I ascertain that, to our eyes, you represent a bank vault - a source of inspiration for our development and shaping our future.
